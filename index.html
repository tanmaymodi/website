FAQ

  • Founding and Develpoment

    All the IIIT are started by MHRD to create a deep technological base in India. Initially, these institutes will be running under PPP Mode. But some of them are completely managed by MHRD. There is partership between the Industries, State Government and Central Government.

  • Industrial Colaboration

    All the IIITs will have collaborated with the Industries which will provide them financial support as well as give their students an opportunity to learn experience from their firm.

  • Department

    In most of the IIIT, only a few branches are there which are Computer Science, Information Technology, Electrical Engineering to create a strong base in Technolgy and focused on the innovation on Advanced Development for the Country.
